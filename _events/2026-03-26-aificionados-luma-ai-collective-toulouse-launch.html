The AI Collective is coming to Toulouse.

After building a strong community of AI founders and engineers in Paris, we're gathering the Toulouse AI ecosystem for a relaxed evening of demos, founder intros, and conversations — hosted together with Dust.

The AI Collective was originally founded in the San Francisco Bay Area by Stanford, YC and big tech alumni. Today the community brings together AI founders, engineers, researchers, operators and investors across major tech hubs to help the next generation of AI startups emerge and scale.

This meetup is about connecting the builders of the local ecosystem and seeing what people are actually working on.

🗓️ Agenda (6:30 PM → 10:00 PM)
6:30 PM — Doors open + welcome drink
7:00 PM — Opening remarks : The AI Collective & community intro.
7:10 PM — Dust presentation & demo
7:30 PM — Startup demos (5 minutes each) : Selected Toulouse AI startups.
8:00 PM — Quick-fire builder intros (1–2 minutes) : Open mic for projects and ideas.
8:20 PM — Drinks, food & open networking
10:00 PM — End of event
